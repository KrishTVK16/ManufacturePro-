CNC Machining Services

Precision machining solutions with advanced technology and exceptional quality

Overview

Our CNC machining services utilize state-of-the-art multi-axis machines to produce complex parts and components with exceptional precision. Whether you need single prototypes or high-volume production runs, we deliver consistent quality and accuracy that meets your exact specifications.

Key Features

  • Multi-Axis Machining: 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis CNC machines for complex geometries and intricate designs that require precise multi-dimensional machining.
  • Tight Tolerances: We achieve tolerances as tight as Â±0.0005" (0.013mm), ensuring your parts meet the most demanding precision requirements.
  • Material Expertise: Extensive experience with metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass) and plastics (ABS, PEEK, Delrin) for diverse applications.
  • Surface Finishing: Multiple surface finish options including anodizing, powder coating, polishing, and specialized treatments to meet your aesthetic and functional needs.

Our Process

1

Design Review

We review your CAD files and specifications to ensure manufacturability and optimize the machining process.

2

Programming & Setup

Our engineers create optimized CNC programs and set up the appropriate machines and tooling for your project.

3

Precision Machining

Parts are machined with precision using advanced CNC equipment, with real-time monitoring for quality assurance.

4

Quality Inspection

Every part undergoes rigorous inspection using CMM systems and other metrology equipment to ensure compliance.

5

Finishing & Delivery

Final surface finishing is applied as specified, followed by careful packaging and timely delivery to your facility.
